About Kavih Photography

Kavih is a photography company based in Coastal Kenya. We have worked with clients from all over the country. In the year 2021, Kavih was named the best photography company during the national annual art awards. We do photo and video coverage for various events including but not limited to:

    Engagements

    Weddings

    Baby showers

    Birthdays

    Graduations

    Funerals
